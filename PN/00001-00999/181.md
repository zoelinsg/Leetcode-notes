# LeetCode

## [181. Employees Earning More Than Their Managers](https://leetcode.com/problems/employees-earning-more-than-their-managers/)

### MySQL

```sql
SELECT e.name AS Employee
FROM Employee e
JOIN Employee m
    ON e.managerId = m.id
WHERE e.salary > m.salary;
```

---

### MSSQL

```sql
SELECT e.name AS Employee
FROM Employee e
JOIN Employee m
    ON e.managerId = m.id
WHERE e.salary > m.salary;
```
